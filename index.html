New to gaming? Look no further

A small guide of reccomended games to try for new players

Intro

Many people are often shy to start gaming simply because they don't know where to start. Here are my personal reccomendations for games you could give a try to get a taste of what lies further. There will be a decent variety of games (All of which I have played) to pick from and test out! I hope this helps you out!

Minecraft

(Insert Image)

Minecraft is a great game to start out with. Available on almost all platforms, it's very easy to get started. You can play Survival mode, which allows you to gather resources and craft items in order to stay safe during your adventure. There is also a Creative mode which allows you to build whatever you want with unlimited resources. You can also play on Servers which allow you to connect with people all over the world and play various minigames and other fun activities!

Personal Rating: 10/10
